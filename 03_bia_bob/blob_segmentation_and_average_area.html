Blob Segmentation Notebook#

This notebook loads a brightâ€‘field image containing synthetic blobs, segments the blobs using simple thresholding and connected component labeling, measures the area of each detected object, and prints the average area.

Steps#

  1. Import required libraries.

  2. Load the image data/blobs.tif.

  3. Threshold the image to obtain a binary mask.
